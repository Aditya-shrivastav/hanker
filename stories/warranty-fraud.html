Warranty claim fraud is a silent drain on enterprise profitability. Across industries, organizations lose millions each year to inflated, manipulated, or entirely fraudulent warranty claims.

At Source Node Technologies, we set out to solve this problem not just with advanced models - but with a system that could be trusted, adopted, and scaled across the organization.

The Challenge

Traditional warranty fraud detection relies heavily on manual checks, rigid rules, and retrospective audits.

This creates three persistent problems:

  • Fraud is detected after payouts are made
  • Claim officers depend on IT or data science teams for analysis
  • Operational teams are overwhelmed by false positives

What organizations needed was a system that could identify risk early, scale across large claim volumes, and empower business users directly.

The Solution: AI-Driven Fraud Detection on Databricks

We built a Warranty Claim Fraud Detection Model, deployed on Databricks, using proven classical AI and machine learning techniques.

Instead of relying on opaque black-box systems, our approach focused on transparency, explainability, and operational usability.

Empowering Claim Officers

One of our primary goals was to remove dependency on IT and data science teams for day-to-day fraud detection.

The solution includes an intuitive user interface that allows claim officers to:

  • Understand why a claim is flagged
  • Explore risk factors visually
  • Make confident, data-driven decisions independently
Fraud detection works best when intelligence is placed directly in the hands of the people making decisions.

Delivering Massive ROI

By identifying fraudulent claims before payout, the system delivers immediate and measurable financial impact.

Classical AI & ML techniques - when applied thoughtfully - have proven highly effective in:

  • Reducing financial leakage
  • Lowering false-positive rates
  • Improving operational efficiency

In real deployments, organizations have saved millions in prevented losses, turning fraud detection from a cost center into a value driver.

Built to Adapt and Scale

No two organizations face the same fraud patterns. That's why the framework was designed to be highly customizable.

It can evolve from a focused warranty fraud solution into a broader anomaly detection platform, covering:

  • Supplier fraud
  • Operational anomalies
  • Process deviations
